3 Ways To Donate

  1. Drop off your donation at the store. We accept donations Tuesday through Satrday from 9:00AM to 5:30PM
  2. Schedule a donation pickup online. You can schedule a donation pickup online here
  3. Call or text our donation coordinator Ien at 214-598-0090


All donation must be in sellable conditions. No rips, tears stains, etc. All appliances must be in working order. For further questions, please call us at 972-548-9112
